## Usage

### Visible

Allow the content to overflow the container.

<Preview className="bg-white o-auto">
  <div className="bw-1 bc-slate bs-d h-32 o-v p-2 w-64">
    <div className="h-64 c-slate w-full">Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall. Implacable November weather. As much mud in the streets as if the waters had but newly retired from the face of the earth.</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "o-v"
<div class="bw-1 bc-slate bs-d h-32 o-v p-2 w-64">
  <div class="h-64 c-slate w-full">Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall. Implacable November weather. As much mud in the streets as if the waters had but newly retired from the face of the earth.</div>
</div>
```
