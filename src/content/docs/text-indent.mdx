## 0

> Initial value

The text will have no indentation at the start of the paragraph.

  <p className="fs-lg c-slate ti-0">{bleakHouse}</p>
</Preview>

<p class="fs-lg c-slate ti-0">London. Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall. Implacable November weather.
