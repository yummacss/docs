## Usage

```html
<div class="ta-auto ...">Full touch support</div>
<div class="ta-none ...">Touch disabled</div>
<div class="ta-px ...">Scroll horizontally only</div>
<div class="ta-py ...">Scroll vertically only</div>
<div class="ta-pr ...">Swipe right only</div>
<div class="ta-pz ...">Pinch to zoom</div>
<div class="ta-m ...">Pan and pinch, no double-tap zoom</div>
```
