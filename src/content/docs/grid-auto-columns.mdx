## Usage

### Auto

Size columns based on the content and available space.

<Preview>
  <div className="d-g gac-auto g-4 gtr-auto">
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4 c-white">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</div>
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">B</div>
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">C</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "gac-auto"
<div class="d-g gac-auto g-4 gtr-auto">
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4 c-white">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</div>
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">B</div>
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">C</div>
</div>
```

### Min Content

Size columns to the smallest possible width without overflowing.

<Preview>
  <div className="d-g gac-min g-4 gtr-auto">
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4 c-white">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</div>
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">B</div>
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">C</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "gac-min"
<div class="d-g gac-min g-4 gtr-auto">
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4 c-white">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</div>

  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">C</div>
</div>
```
