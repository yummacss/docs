## Auto

> Initial value

The columns will size themselves based on the content and available space in the grid container.

<Preview>
  <div className="d-g gac-auto g-4 gtr-auto">
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4 c-white">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</div>
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">B</div>
    <div className="ai-c bg-indigo-8 d-f jc-c p-4 c-indigo-5">C</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

## Min Content

The columns will size themselves to the smallest possible width that does not cause overflow.
