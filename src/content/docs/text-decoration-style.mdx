## Solid

> Initial value

The text will be displayed with a solid line for its decoration.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-u tds-s">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tds-s"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-u tds-s">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

Override existing utilities based on the user's [screen size](/docs/media-queries) or other factors, such as [hover states](/docs/pseudo-classes).

Target different screen sizes by adding breakpoint prefixes. Styles apply from the specified breakpoint and up.
