## Solid

> Initial value

The text will be displayed with a solid line for its decoration.

<Preview className="bg-white">
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate tdl-u tds-s">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tds-s"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate tdl-u tds-s">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
