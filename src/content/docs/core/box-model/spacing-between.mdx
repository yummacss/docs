## Spacing X

Controls the left and right X-axis margins of child elements.

<Utility classPrefix="s-x-" incrementValue={0.25} propNames={["margin-left", "margin-right"]} range={100} unit="rem" />

This example sets the left and right margins of child elements to **2rem**. The left margin is calculated as `calc(2rem * (1 - 0))`, resulting in **2rem**, while the right margin is calculated as `calc(2rem * 0)`, resulting in **0**. This results in a uniform spacing of **2rem** between child elements.

```html live "s-x-8"
<div class="d-if fd-r stripes s-x-8">
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f dim-16 jc-c rad-1 tc-white">A</div>
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f dim-16 jc-c rad-1 tc-white">B</div>
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f dim-16 jc-c rad-1 tc-white">C</div>
</div>
```

## Spacing Y

Controls the bottom and top Y-axis margins of child elements.

<Utility classPrefix="s-y-" incrementValue={0.25} propNames={["margin-bottom", "margin-top"]} range={100} unit="rem" />

This example sets the bottom and top margins of child elements to **2rem**. The bottom margin is calculated as `calc(2rem * 0)`, resulting in **0**, while the top margin is calculated as `calc(2rem * (1 - 0))`, resulting in **2rem**. This creates a uniform spacing of **2rem** between child elements.

```html live "s-y-8"
<div class="d-if fd-c stripes s-y-8">
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f dim-16 jc-c rad-1 tc-white">A</div>
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f dim-16 jc-c rad-1 tc-white">B</div>
  <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f dim-16 jc-c rad-1 tc-white">C</div>
</div>
```
