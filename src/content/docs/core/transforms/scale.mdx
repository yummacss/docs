This example showcases various scaling transformations:

- The **scale-70** utility scales the element to **70%** of its original size.
- The **scale-80** utility scales the element to **80%** of its original size.
- The **scale-90** utility scales the element to **90%** of its original size.

```html live
<style>
  @import url("https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/yumma-lib/yumma-css@release/dist/yumma.css");
</style>
<div class="d-g g-16 gtc-1 sm:gtc-3">
  <img class="d-24 rad-1 t-s-70" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
  <img class="d-24 rad-1 t-s-80" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
  <img class="d-24 rad-1 t-s-90" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
</div>
```
