This example showcases various scaling transformations:

- The **scale-70** utility scales the element to **70%** of its original size.
- The **scale-80** utility scales the element to **80%** of its original size.
- The **scale-90** utility scales the element to **90%** of its original size.

```html live
<style>
  @import url("https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/yumma-lib/yumma-css@release/dist/yumma.css");
</style>
<div class="d-g g-16 gtc-1 sm:gtc-3">
  <div class="my-4 p-r">
    <img class="d-18 d-ib rad-1 t-s-70 t-r-30" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
    <img class="o-20 d-18 d-ib l-0 p-a rad-1" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
  </div>
  <div class="my-4 p-r">
    <img class="d-18 d-ib rad-1 t-s-80 t-r-30" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
    <img class="o-20 d-18 d-ib l-0 p-a rad-1" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
  </div>
  <div class="my-4 p-r">
    <img class="d-18 d-ib rad-1 t-s-90 t-r-30" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
    <img class="o-20 d-18 d-ib l-0 p-a rad-1" src="https://picsum.photos/600?image=360" />
  </div>
</div>
```
