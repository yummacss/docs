Controls how the background responds to scrolling.

<Class name="/background-attachment" />

## Fixed

The background doesn't change, and it's always in the same position relative to the viewport.

{/* prettier-ignore */}
```html live "ba-f"
<div class="ba-f bc-b bp-c bs-c h-32 ovf-y-s rad-2 w-full" style='background-image: url("/assets/images/mountain.png");'>
  <p class="fs-3xl fw-600 p-6 ta-c tc-white">
    "If you love somebody. Better tell them while they're here 'cause. They just
    may run away from you"
  </p>
</div>
```

## Local

The background doesn't change, even when the contents of the element change.

{/* prettier-ignore */}
```html live "ba-l"
<div class="ba-l bc-b bp-c bs-c h-32 ovf-y-s rad-2 w-full" style='background-image: url("/assets/images/mountain.png");'>
  <p class="fs-3xl fw-600 p-6 ta-c tc-white">
    "If you love somebody. Better tell them while they're here 'cause. They just
    may run away from you"
  </p>
</div>
```

## Scroll

The background doesn't change when you scroll, and it doesn't move with the contents of the element.

{/* prettier-ignore */}
```html live "ba-s"
<div class="ba-s bc-b bp-c bs-c h-32 ovf-y-s rad-2 w-full" style='background-image: url("/assets/images/mountain.png");'>
  <p class="fs-3xl fw-600 p-6 ta-c tc-white">
    "If you love somebody. Better tell them while they're here 'cause. They just
    may run away from you"
  </p>
</div>
```
