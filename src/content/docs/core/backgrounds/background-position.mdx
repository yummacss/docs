## Combining utilities

This example showcases various **background position** styles: `top`, `bottom`, `left`, `right`, `center`, and their combinations such as `left top`, `left bottom`, `right top`, and `right bottom`. Each section demonstrates how the background image is positioned within the element, allowing you to see how different properties affect the placement of the image.

```html live "bp-lt" "bp-t" "bp-rt" "bp-l" "bp-c" "bp-r" "bp-lb" "bp-b" "bp-rb"
<style>
  @import url("https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/yumma-lib/yumma-css@release/dist/yumma.css");
</style>
<div class="d-g g-1 gtc-3 p-r rad-1">
  <div class="bp-lt d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-t d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-rt d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-l d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-c d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-r d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-lb d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-b d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
  <div class="bp-rb d-24 p-r rad-1" style="background-image:url(https://picsum.photos/id/870/300/300)"></div>
</div>
```
