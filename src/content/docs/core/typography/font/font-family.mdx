## Charter

This example sets the font family to **Charter**. If Charter is not available, it will fall back to **Cambria**, and then to a generic **serif** font.

```html live "ff-c" layout="/src/layouts/wide.astro"
<div class="bg-l-indigo-6 p-4 rad-1">
  <p class="bg-white ff-c fs-sm p-4 rad-1 ta-c tc-lead">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
</div>
```

```html live "ff-m" layout="/src/layouts/wide.astro"
<div class="bg-l-indigo-6 p-4 rad-1">
  <p class="bg-white ff-m fs-sm p-4 rad-1 ta-c tc-lead">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
```
