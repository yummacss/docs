This example showcases various text colors using the **indigo** color palette:

- The **tc-l-indigo-4** utility applies a lighter indigo shade.
- The **tc-indigo** utility applies the base indigo shade.
- The **tc-d-indigo-2** utility applies a darker indigo shade.

<Decorator name="input_select">Try writing a sentence to see the caret color.</Decorator>

```html live "tc-l-indigo-4" "tc-indigo" "tc-d-indigo-2" layout="/src/layouts/wide.astro"
<div class="bg-l-indigo-6 p-4 rad-1">
  <div class="bg-white d-g p-4 rad-1 rg-4">
    <p class="ta-c tc-l-indigo-4">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p class="ta-c tc-indigo">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
    <p class="ta-c tc-d-indigo-2">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</p>
  </div>
</div>
```
