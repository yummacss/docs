## 100

This weight is very light and is typically used for fine text.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-100 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 200

This weight is light and is often used for subtle emphasis.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-200 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 300

This weight is slightly bolder than light and is used for emphasis.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-300 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 400

> Initial value

This is the normal weight for body text.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-400 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 500

This weight is medium and is often used for headings or important text.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-500 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 600

This weight is semi-bold and is typically used for prominent headings.

<Note icon="heart">Yumma CSS uses the value `600` for all headings. Learn more about it in the [base styles docs](/docs/base-styles).</Note>

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-600 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 700

This weight is bold and is often used for major headings.

<Note icon="heart">Yumma CSS uses the value `700` for elements like `<b>` and `<strong>`. Learn more about it in the [base styles docs](/docs/base-styles).</Note>

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-700 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 800

This weight is extra bold and is typically used for very prominent text.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fw-800 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

## 900

This weight is the heaviest and is often used for the most impactful text.

  <p className="fs-lg fw-900 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
