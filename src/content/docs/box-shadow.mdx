This example showcases various `box-shadow` utilities:

- The **bsh-xs** box shadow utility applies an extra small shadow to an element.
- The **bsh-sm** box shadow utility applies a small shadow to an element.
- The **bsh-md** box shadow utility applies a medium shadow to an element.
- Finally, **bsh-lg** box shadow utility applies a large to an element.
