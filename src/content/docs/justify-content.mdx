## Usage

### Normal

Align items according to the container's default alignment.

<Preview variant="inline">
    <div className="d-f g-4 jc-n c-white" id="area">
      <div className="d-f ai-c jc-c w-16 h-16 bg-indigo">A</div>
      <div className="d-f ai-c jc-c w-16 h-16 bg-indigo">B</div>
      <div className="d-f ai-c jc-c w-16 h-16 bg-indigo">C</div>
    </div>
</Preview>

```html "jc-n"
<div class="d-f g-4 jc-n c-white">
  <div class="d-f ai-c jc-c w-16 h-16 bg-indigo">A</div>
  <div class="d-f ai-c jc-c w-16 h-16 bg-indigo">B</div>
  <div class="d-f ai-c jc-c w-16 h-16 bg-indigo">C</div>
</div>
```

</Preview>


### Stretch

Stretch items to fill the available space along the main axis.

<Preview variant="inline">
    <div className="c-white" id="area">
      <div className="d-g g-4 gaf-c jc-s">
          <div className="d-f ai-c jc-c h-14 bg-indigo">A</div>
          <div className="d-f ai-c jc-c h-14 bg-indigo">B</div>
          <div className="d-f ai-c jc-c h-14 bg-indigo">C</div>
      </div>
    </div>
</Preview>

```html "jc-s"
<div class="c-white">
  <div class="d-g g-4 gaf-c jc-s">
    <div class="d-f ai-c jc-c h-14 bg-indigo">A</div>
    <div class="d-f ai-c jc-c h-14 bg-indigo">B</div>
    <div class="d-f ai-c jc-c h-14 bg-indigo">C</div>
  </div>
</div>
```
