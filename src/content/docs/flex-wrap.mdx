## Usage

### No Wrap

Prevent items from wrapping to the next line.

<Preview>
  <div className="p-r c-white">
    <div className="d-f fw-nw g-4 max-w-30" id="area">
      <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">A</div>
      <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">B</div>
      <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">C</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "fw-nw"
<div class="p-r c-white">
  <div class="d-f fw-nw g-4 max-w-30">
    <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">A</div>
    <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">B</div>
    <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">C</div>
  </div>
</div>
```

### Wrap

Allow items to wrap onto multiple lines.

<Preview>
  <div className="p-r c-white">
    <div className="d-f fw-w g-4 max-w-30" id="area">
      <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">A</div>
      <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">B</div>
      <div className="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">C</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "fw-w"
<div class="p-r c-white">
  <div class="d-f fw-w g-4 max-w-30">
    <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">A</div>
    <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">B</div>
    <div class="ai-c bg-indigo d-f jc-c p-4">C</div>
  </div>
</div>
```
