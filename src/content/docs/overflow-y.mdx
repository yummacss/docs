## Usage

### Visible

Allow the content to overflow the container vertically.

<Preview className="o-auto bg-white">
  <div className="o-y-v h-32 p-2 w-64 bc-slate bw-1 bs-d">
    <div className="h-64 w-full c-slate">Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall. Implacable November weather. As much mud in the streets as if the waters had but newly retired from the face of the earth.</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "o-y-v"
<div class="o-y-v h-32 p-2 w-64 bc-slate bw-1 bs-d">
  <div class="h-64 w-full c-slate">Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall. Implacable November weather. As much mud in the streets as if the waters had but newly retired from the face of the earth.</div>
</div>
```
