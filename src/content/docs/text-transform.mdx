## None

> Initial value

The text will be displayed as it is, without any transformation applied.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-none"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Capitalize

The first letter of each word will be converted to uppercase.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-c">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-c"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-c">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Lowercase

All letters in the text will be converted to lowercase.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-l">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-l"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-l">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Uppercase

All letters in the text will be converted to uppercase.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-u"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tt-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
