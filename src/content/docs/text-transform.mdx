## Usage

### None

> Initial value

<Preview>
  <p className="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-none"
<p class="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

### Capitalize

<Preview>
  <p className="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-c">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-c"
<p class="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-c">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

### Lowercase

<Preview>
  <p className="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-l">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-l"
<p class="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-l">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

### Uppercase

<Preview>
  <p className="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tt-u"
<p class="c-slate fs-lg ta-c tt-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
