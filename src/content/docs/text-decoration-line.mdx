## Line Through

The text will be displayed with a line through it, indicating that it is struck out.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-lt">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tdl-lt"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-lt">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## None

The text will be displayed without any decoration lines.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tdl-none"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Underline

The text will be displayed with an underline, emphasizing the content.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tdl-u"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Overline

The text will be displayed with a line above it.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-o">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tdl-o"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate tdl-o">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
