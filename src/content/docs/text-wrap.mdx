## Wrap

> Initial value

The text will wrap to the next line when it reaches the end of the container.

<Preview>
  <div className="d-g g-4 max-w-64 mx-auto">
    <div className="fs-xl fw-600 c-slate tw-w">Bleak House by Charles Dickens</div>
    <div className="fs-sm c-gray-7">{bleakHouse}</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "tw-w"
<div class="d-g g-4 max-w-64 mx-auto">
  <div class="fs-xl fw-600 c-slate tw-w">Bleak House by Charles Dickens</div>
  <div class="fs-sm c-gray-7">
    London. Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall...
  </div>
</div>
```

## Balance

The text will be wrapped in a way that balances the line lengths, creating a more visually appealing layout.

<Preview>

  </div>

    London. Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall...
  </div>

## No Wrap

The text will not wrap to the next line, and it will overflow the container if it exceeds the width.

<Preview>
  <div className="d-g g-4 max-w-64 mx-auto">
    <div className="fs-xl fw-600 c-slate tw-nw">Bleak House by Charles Dickens</div>
    <div className="fs-sm c-gray-7">{bleakHouse}</div>
  </div>
</Preview>

```html "tw-nw"
<div class="d-g g-4 max-w-64 mx-auto">
  <div class="fs-xl fw-600 c-slate tw-nw">Bleak House by Charles Dickens</div>
  <div class="fs-sm c-gray-7">
    London. Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall...
  </div>
</div>
```
