## Usage

```html
<p class="us-auto ...">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
<p class="us-a ...">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
<p class="us-none ...">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
<p class="us-t ...">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
