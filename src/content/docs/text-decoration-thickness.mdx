## Auto

> Initial value

The text will have a default decoration thickness.

<Preview className="bg-white">
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate tdl-u tdt-auto">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "tdt-auto"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate tdl-u tdt-auto">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
