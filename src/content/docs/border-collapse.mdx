## Separate

> Initial value

The borders of the table cells will remain distinct, allowing for spacing between them.

## Collapse

The borders of the table will be merged together.

<Preview>
  <table className="bw-1 bc-gray bc-c ta-c c-gray w-full">
    <tbody>

      </tr>
      <tr>

      </tr>
    </tbody>
  </table>
