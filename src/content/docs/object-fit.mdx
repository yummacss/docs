## Usage

### Fill

Stretch the content to fill the entire container.

<Preview>
  <div className="h-40 w-80">
```html
<div class="h-40 w-80">
  <img class="of-f w-full h-full" src="..." />
</div>
<div class="h-40 w-80">
  <img class="of-c w-full h-full" src="..." />
</div>
<div class="h-40 w-80">
  <img class="of-none w-full h-full" src="..." />
</div>
<div class="h-40 w-80">
  <img class="of-sd w-full h-full" src="..." />
</div>
```
