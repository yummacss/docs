## Scroll

Enables a horizontal scrollbar for the container, allowing users to scroll through the content even if it doesn’t overflow.

<Preview className="bg-white">
    <div className="b-1 bc-black p-2 h-32 o-x-s w-64">
      <div className="tc-black w-96">Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall. Implacable November weather. As much mud in the streets as if the waters had but newly retired from the face of the earth.</div>
    </div>
</Preview>

```html "o-x-s"
<div class="b-1 bc-black h-32 o-x-s w-64">
  <div class="tc-black w-96">This content overflows the container, and a horizontal scrollbar is always visible.</div>
</div>
```
