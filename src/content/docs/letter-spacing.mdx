<ApiTable category="text" name="letter-spacing" />

## 0

The letters will be spaced normally.

```html live "ls-0" layout="/src/layouts/inline.astro"
<div class="bg-indigo-1 p-4 rad-1">
  <p class="bg-white fs-lg ls-0 p-4 rad-1 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</div>
```

## 1

The letters will be slightly closer together than normal.

```html live "ls-1" layout="/src/layouts/inline.astro"
<div class="bg-indigo-1 p-4 rad-1">
  <p class="bg-white fs-lg ls-1 p-4 rad-1 ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</div>
```

## 2

The letters will be slightly closer together.


## 3

