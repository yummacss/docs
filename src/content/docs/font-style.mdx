## Normal

> Initial value

The text will be displayed in a standard format without any italicization.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fs-n ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "fs-n"
<p class="fs-lg fs-n ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Italic

The text will be displayed in an italicized format.

<Preview className="bg-white" variant="inline">
  <p className="fs-lg fs-i ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "fs-i"
<p class="fs-lg fs-i ta-c tc-slate">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
