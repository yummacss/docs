## Usage

### Scroll

The background image moves with the page content.

<Hint icon="mouse_scroll">Scroll through the image to see its behavior.</Hint>

<Preview>
    <div className="o-auto ba-s bs-c ff-c fs-xxl fw-700 max-h-80 p-5 ta-c" style={{backgroundImage: 'url(/img/sea.jpg)'}}>{bleakHouse}</div>
</Preview>

```html "ba-s"
<div class="o-auto ba-s bs-c ff-c fs-xxl fw-700 max-h-80 p-5 ta-c" style="background-image: url(/img/sea.jpg);">
  London. Michaelmas term lately over, and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall.
</div>
```
