## None

The text will be displayed without any decoration.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate td-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "td-none"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate td-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Underline

The text will be displayed with an underline.

<Preview>
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c c-slate td-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "td-u"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c c-slate td-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
