## None

The text will be displayed without any decoration.

<Preview className="bg-white">
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate td-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "td-none"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate td-none">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```

## Underline

The text will be displayed with an underline.

<Preview className="bg-white">
  <p className="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate td-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
</Preview>

```html "td-u"
<p class="fs-lg ta-c tc-slate td-u">Sphinx of black quartz, judge my vow.</p>
```
