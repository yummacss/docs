## Usage

```html
<p class="ta-s ...">Start</p>
<p class="ta-c ...">Center</p>
<p class="ta-e ...">End</p>
<p class="ta-j ...">Justify</p>
<p class="ta-ja ...">Justify All</p>
<p class="ta-l ...">Left</p>
<p class="ta-r ...">Right</p>
<p class="ta-mp ...">Match Parent</p>
```
