## What's included?

Every template comes with everything you need to get started. Here's what you can expect:

- **Multiple pages** - Each template includes several pre-built pages like Home, About, Services, and more.
- **Responsive design** - All templates are fully responsive and look great on any device.
- **Clean code** - Built with TypeScript and best practices, so you can easily understand and customize.
- **Lifetime updates** - Buy once, get updates forever. No subscriptions.

---

## What's the tech stack?

All templates are built with modern technologies:

- **Yumma CSS** for styling
- **Next.js** for server-side rendering
- **React** as frontend framework
- **pnpm** for package management
- **TypeScript** for type safety

Since everything is built on top of [Yumma CSS](/docs), you can easily customize any template to match your brand.
