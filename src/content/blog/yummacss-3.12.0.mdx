## CommonJS support

While ESM is the future of JavaScript, many projects still rely on CommonJS. Supporting both formats makes Yumma CSS more accessible & easier to integrate into any workflow.

CJS support is added to these packages:

- [cli](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@yummacss/cli)
- [nitro](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@yummacss/nitro)
- [runtime](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@yummacss/runtime)

This update benefits projects & tools that rely on CommonJS for build processes or development environments.
